ReelPhotos User Guide

1. Introduction

Welcome to ReelPhotos, an immersive multi-video player designed to revolutionize how you interact with your saved portrait-oriented videos. With a unique tri-screen interface, ReelPhotos offers seamless navigation through your entire video library, allowing you to swipe effortlessly between your stored videos. Our vision is to enhance your media experience by providing an intuitive and convenient platform that brings your memories to life.

2. Getting Started

Downloading the App

ReelPhotos is available on the App Store. To download:

  • Open the App Store on your device.
  • Search for ReelPhotos.
  • Tap the Download button to install the app.

Initial Setup

Upon launching ReelPhotos for the first time:

  • Permissions: The app will request access to your photo library. Tap Allow to enable the app to access your videos. This permission is essential for the app to function properly.
  • Library Scanning: ReelPhotos will scan your photo library to load your videos. If you have a large library, this may take some time. Please be patient and allow the app to complete the scanning process.

3. Key Features

Multi-Video Player

Experience seamless navigation with our swipable interface:

  • Swipe Left/Right: Move back and forth between your videos stored locally or in your iCloud library.
  • Individual or Unified Swipe Modes: Choose your preferred navigation style for a personalized experience.

Reel Viewer

Create personalized playlists by playing back entire albums:

  • Select Albums: Choose any album from your photo library to view its videos as a reel.
  • Customization: Organize your videos into albums for easy access and tailored playback.

Additional Features

  • Tap-to-Mute Functionality: Tap the screen to mute or unmute the video audio.
  • Double-Tap for Slow Motion: Double-tap on the video to activate slow-motion playback, allowing you to catch every detail.

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I add videos to the multi-video player?
    ReelPhotos automatically plays videos from your albums. To add videos:
    • Add to Albums: Place your desired videos into an album within your photo library. ReelPhotos will recognize and include them in the playback.
  • What video formats are supported?
    ReelPhotos supports standard video formats compatible with iOS devices, including:
    • MP4
    • MOV
    • M4V
  • Can I use ReelPhotos offline?
    Yes, you can use ReelPhotos offline:
    • Local Storage: When offline, the app will play videos stored on your device.
    • iCloud Library: Videos in your iCloud library require an internet connection and wonâ€™t be accessible offline.
  • Troubleshooting Tips:
    • App Crashing or Freezing:
      • Ensure your device is running the latest iOS version.
      • Update ReelPhotos to the latest version.
      • Restart the app or your device.
    • Videos Not Loading:
      • Confirm that ReelPhotos has permission to access your photo library under Settings > Privacy > Photos.
    • Slow Performance with Large Libraries:
      • Loading large libraries may cause slowdowns.
      • Allow the app adequate time to scan and load your entire library.
